About Us

We're the Swim Dads, a Toronto rock band that got its start where all great bands do, at our kids' swim classes.

We've been gigging around Toronto and the GTA since February 2023, playing a mix of originals and covers for fun, good vibes, and the occasional payday.

Our musical heart beats to the sound of '90s and 2000s rock and blues, but we're not afraid to wander, our set list hops across genres.
